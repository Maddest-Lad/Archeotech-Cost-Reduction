Provides two new technologies to reduce the cost of Archeotech Components.

[b] Relic Ship Integration [/b]
[list]
[*] Tier 4 Rare Voidcraft Tech
[*] Reduces Minor Artifact Cost by 40% for Archeotech Components 
[/list]

[b] Improved Relic Integration [/b]
[list]
[*] Repeatable Voidcraft Tech
[*] Repeatable Up to 8 Times
[*] Reduces Minor Artifact Cost by 5% for Archeotech Components
[/list]

Prerequisites: Archaeoengineers Ascension Perk
Required DLC: Ancient Relics Story Pack

Mod Source: https://github.com/Maddest-Lad/Stellaris-Archeotech-Cost-Reduction
